(Screenshot)

Click “data backup”

(Screenshot)

Select date for data backup is required. 
(Screenshot)

click “Select range”
(Screenshot)

select date from “Custom range” to export the data for specific date.
(Screenshot)

Click “apply”
(Screenshot)

Click “export” 
(Screenshot)

Your data back up has been saved in your system setting.
